Mental health refers to cognitive, behavioral, and emotional well-being.
"Mental health" to mean the absence of a mental disorder that issue mostly seen in older age people
Conditions such as stress, depression, and anxiety can all affect one's mental health.
According to a Centers for Disease Control (CDC) study of mental health in older adults aged 65+, it is estimated that 20% of seniors experience some type of mental health concern
